Cornerstone Charity

Bright Future for Underprivileged Children in Kenya

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged but bright children in Kenya by providing food, shelter, and education, unlocking their full potential and creating a brighter future for them and their communities.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every child, regardless of background, has equal access to basic needs and quality education.

About Us

Cornerstone Charity is a grassroots organization committed to identifying, supporting, and nurturing academically talented but financially challenged children across Kenya. We work with communities, volunteers, and partners to deliver essential programs that change lives.
